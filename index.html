Hi, I'm @Person20020

I am a highschooler in the United States. I enjoy programming, tinkering with electronics, and designing and 3D printing parts for variouos projects.


3D Printing

I have been in the hobby of 3D printing for about two years at this point, and now have two printers. I started out with an ancient 3rd hand Monoprice Maker Select that by now is probably about 10 years old. I also have a Prusa Mini+ that I got about a year ago and is sooo much better.

I mostly use 3D printing for functional prints like parts for my projects or replacement parts.


Keyboards

I am also very interested in custom keyboards. This is one of the main things I use 3D printing for. I made my own lily58 layout keyboard, and have also made macropads and a number pad as well.


Programming

I also enjoy programming. I am part of Hack Club, a organization that does coding and other events for middle and highschoolers. One of my favorite events that I have participated in was Hackpad, a program where you designed a PCB, case, and firmware for a custom macro pad and then got all of the parts manufactured and shipped to you. The event is currently in its second round and I am working on another custom 3x6 column staggered split keyboard.
